At TheBrave Finance, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your financial and personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our integrated financial services, including MT5 access, managed trading, and VIP signals.

analytics 1. Data Collection

We collect various types of information to provide a secure and personalized trading experience. This data is categorized as follows:

person_search
Personal Data

Name, address, email, phone number, and government-issued identification for KYC/AML compliance.

account_balance_wallet
Financial Data

Transaction history, wallet addresses, trading preferences, and source of funds documentation.

devices
Technical Data

IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and usage patterns on our MT5 and web platforms.

memory 2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for specific purposes to maintain service integrity and legal compliance:

  • check_circle To facilitate and manage your trading accounts and financial transactions.
  • check_circle To perform required identity verification and anti-money laundering (AML) checks.
  • check_circle To provide personalized VIP trading signals based on your risk profile.
  • check_circle To communicate critical updates regarding platform maintenance or security alerts.

cookie 3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide secure login sessions. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings; however, disabling certain cookies may impact the functionality of our trading terminal.

info
Our cookies do not store sensitive financial data. They are primarily used for performance monitoring and session management to prevent unauthorized access to your account.

partner_reports 4. Third-party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with trusted partners only when necessary:

  • arrow_forward Liquidity Providers: To execute trades and provide market access.
  • arrow_forward Regulators: When required by law or to comply with legal processes (e.g., FINRA, SEC).
  • arrow_forward Verification Services: For automated KYC and AML background checks.

gavel 5. User Rights (GDPR & CCPA Compliance)

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have certain rights regarding your personal data:

01
Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

02
Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten")

Request deletion of your data, subject to regulatory retention requirements.

03
Right to Portability

Obtain your data in a structured, commonly used format.

lock_reset 6. Security Measures

We implement institutional-grade security protocols, including AES-256 encryption, multi-factor authentication (MFA), and cold storage for digital assets. Our systems are audited regularly to ensure compliance with global cybersecurity standards.
